FCL / LCL

▼

Flexible container shipping solutions.

FCL (Full Container Load) is designed for businesses that require an entire container for their shipment, offering maximum security and faster delivery times. Meanwhile, LCL (Less Container Load) allows multiple shipments from different clients to share container space, making it a more cost-efficient option for smaller cargo volumes. Both options provide flexibility and efficiency, helping businesses optimize their shipping strategy.
